Capabilities

Built for cautious, local awareness.

PulseTrackr is not an emergency dispatch service. It is a community reporting and trusted-contact alert companion.

4 areas

Incident reporting

Submit reports with title, description, category, severity, optional photo, optional voice note, and current location.

Community signals

People can mark incidents as seen, not seen, unsafe, road blocked, or cleared so the feed can update over time.

Privacy controls

Users can hide exact report location, adjust watch radius, and manage location precision through iOS settings.

SOS trusted contacts

Trusted contacts are saved locally and exact location updates are shared only after the user activates SOS.
