App Store Compliance

Last updated: October 10, 2025

Park Memories is designed to comply with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and privacy requirements. As a family photo-sharing app, we prioritize safety, transparency, and user trust. Below, we outline key compliance areas.

Safety (Guideline 1)

Objectionable Content

No support for explicit material. Photos are private to family groups; we moderate for violations.

User-Generated Content

Family feeds are invite-only. We use Firebase rules to restrict access.

Children

Not for users under 13 without consent (COPPA-compliant). Parental controls via account settings.

Performance (Guideline 2)

Minimum Functionality

Core features (camera, filters, sharing) work offline where possible; cloud sync via Firebase.

Accuracy

Location tags are user-approved; no misleading claims.

Data Collection

Only essential data (e.g., geolocation for stamps) with clear prompts.

Business (Guideline 3)

Payments

In-app purchases via Apple; no external billing.

Ads

None; ad-free experience.

User-Generated Content

Private sharing only; no public feeds.

Design (Guideline 4)

Hardware Compatibility

Optimized for iOS 16+; tested on various devices.

Software Requirements

Uses standard APIs (e.g., MapKit for Radar).

UI/UX

Intuitive, accessible design with voiceover support.

Legal (Guideline 5)

Privacy

Full policy linked in App and Store (see Privacy Policy). Data types: Identifiers (email), Geolocation (precise, user-initiated), User Content (photos). No sensitive data or third-party tracking.

Intellectual Property

User content licensed non-exclusively; we respect copyrights.

Developer Identity

Verified; no spam or misleading metadata.

We monitor guidelines updates and adjust accordingly. For questions, contact compliance@edentv.us. Apple review feedback is addressed promptly.
