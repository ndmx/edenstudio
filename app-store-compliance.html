App Store Compliance

Last updated: December 19, 2024

This page outlines our compliance with Apple App Store guidelines and requirements for all EdenTV applications. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality, security, and user experience.

App Store Review Guidelines

All EdenTV applications comply with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines. Our apps are designed to provide value to users while adhering to all platform requirements.

Privacy & Data Collection

Privacy Policy

We maintain a comprehensive Privacy Policy that clearly explains:

  • What data we collect and why
  • How data is used and protected
  • User rights regarding their data
  • Data retention and deletion policies

App Privacy Details

For each app, we provide accurate privacy information in App Store Connect:

  • Data collection practices
  • Data usage purposes
  • Data linked to user identity
  • Data used for tracking

Security & Safety

Data Encryption

All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3+). Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

User Safety

  • Content appropriate for all ages unless specified otherwise
  • No harmful or offensive content
  • Protection of user privacy and personal information
  • Safe user interactions and community features

App Quality & Performance

Technical Requirements

  • Minimum iOS version: iOS 17.0+
  • Optimized for iOS 18+ features
  • Supports all device sizes and orientations
  • Efficient resource usage and battery life

User Experience

  • Intuitive and responsive interface
  • Clear navigation and functionality
  • Accessibility features (VoiceOver, Dynamic Type)
  • Regular updates and bug fixes

Content & Intellectual Property

Original Content

All app content is original or properly licensed. We respect intellectual property rights and comply with copyright laws.

Third-Party Content

  • Clear attribution for third-party assets
  • Proper licensing for all included content
  • No unauthorized use of trademarks or copyrighted material

Business Model Compliance

In-App Purchases

  • Uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for digital goods
  • Clear pricing and subscription terms
  • Easy cancellation and refund processes

Advertising

  • Complies with App Tracking Transparency
  • Respects user privacy choices
  • Age-appropriate advertising content

Legal Compliance

Terms of Service

We maintain comprehensive Terms of Service that cover:

  • User rights and responsibilities
  • Service limitations and disclaimers
  • Dispute resolution procedures
  • Governing law and jurisdiction

Export Compliance

All apps comply with U.S. export control regulations and Apple's encryption requirements.

App-Specific Compliance

ParkMemoryHub

Medical & Health Claims

This app provides cognitive training exercises and memory enhancement techniques. We explicitly state that this is not medical advice and users should consult healthcare professionals.

Data Handling

  • User progress data stored locally on device
  • Optional iCloud sync with user consent
  • No collection of health or medical data
  • Analytics data anonymized and aggregated

Accessibility

  • Full VoiceOver support
  • Dynamic Type compatibility
  • High contrast mode support
  • Reduced motion options

Regular Compliance Reviews

We conduct regular reviews to ensure ongoing compliance:

  • Monthly App Store guideline reviews
  • Quarterly privacy policy updates
  • Annual security audits
  • Continuous monitoring of user feedback

Updates & Changes

When we make significant updates to our apps, we ensure:

  • Updated privacy information in App Store Connect
  • Clear change logs for users
  • Compliance with new App Store requirements
  • Testing on latest iOS versions

Contact & Support

Email: compliance@edentv.us

App Store Support: Contact through App Store review process

Compliance Certification

EdenTV is committed to maintaining Apple Developer Program membership and complying with all current App Store Review Guidelines. This page serves as a public commitment to our compliance standards.

Compliance Statement

All EdenTV applications are developed and maintained in accordance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, Human Interface Guidelines, and all applicable laws and regulations. We regularly review and update our practices to ensure continued compliance.
