Apple App Store Review Guidelines Compliance

EdenTV is committed to complying with all Apple App Store Review Guidelines and requirements. This page outlines our compliance measures and provides transparency about our app development practices.

1. App Store Requirements

Guideline 5.1.1 - Legal Requirements

Privacy Policy: All our apps include a comprehensive privacy policy that discloses data collection, usage, and sharing practices. The privacy policy is available at https://edentv.us/privacy-policy.html.

Guideline 5.1.2 - Data Collection and Storage

Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. Our apps follow Apple's privacy nutrition labels and App Privacy Details requirements.

Data Linked to You

The following data may be linked to your identity:

  • User ID
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Photos and content you create
  • Location data (if enabled)

Data Not Linked to You

We do not collect or track data that is not linked to user identity, such as device information for analytics purposes.

3. Privacy Policy Compliance

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Data Sharing

We may share data with service providers only as necessary to provide our services. We do not sell user data to third parties.

Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their data through the app settings or by contacting us. Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services.

4. App Store Review Guidelines

Guideline 2.1 - App Completeness

Our apps are complete and fully functional. Beta features are clearly marked as such.

Guideline 2.2 - Beta Testing

We use TestFlight for beta testing when applicable and comply with all beta testing guidelines.

Guideline 2.3 - Accurate Metadata

All app descriptions, screenshots, and metadata accurately represent the app's functionality.

Guideline 3.1 - In-App Purchase

Our apps may include in-app purchases that comply with all IAP guidelines.

Guideline 4.1 - Spam

Our apps do not spam users or manipulate reviews.

Guideline 4.2 - Minimum Functionality

All apps provide meaningful functionality and are not simply web clippings.

Guideline 4.3 - Accurate Advertising

App previews and screenshots accurately represent app functionality.

5. Security Measures

Data Encryption

All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest.

Authentication

User accounts use secure authentication methods. We implement proper session management.

Regular Audits

We regularly audit our apps for security vulnerabilities and update dependencies.

6. User Rights

Access and Correction

Users can access their data through app settings and request corrections by contacting us.

Data Portability

Users can export their data in standard formats upon request.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their accounts through the app or by contacting support.

7. Contact Information

For questions about App Store compliance or to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

8. Updates and Changes

We regularly review and update our compliance practices. Material changes will be communicated to users and reflected in app updates.

9. Third-Party Compliance

We ensure all third-party services and SDKs used in our apps comply with App Store guidelines and privacy requirements.
