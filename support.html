Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Restart the application
  2. Check for app updates in the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  5. Check our FAQ section below

Frequently Asked Questions

ParkMemoryHub

How do I backup my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved locally on your device. For additional backup, you can enable iCloud sync in the app settings.

Is the app free to use?

ParkMemoryHub offers a free version with basic features. Premium features are available through in-app purchases.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes, most features work offline. Only cloud sync and some advanced features require an internet connection.

General App Questions

How do I update the app?

Open the App Store, tap your profile picture, and check for available updates. You can also enable automatic updates.

How do I delete my account?

Please contact us directly for account deletion requests. We'll process your request within 30 days.

Do you offer refunds?

For in-app purchases, please contact Apple Support directly as per App Store policies. We'll assist where possible.

Report Issues

When reporting a bug or issue, please include:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version number
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if applicable
  • Date and time the issue occurred

Feature Requests

We love hearing from our users! If you have suggestions for new features or improvements:

  • Email us with detailed descriptions
  • Include how the feature would benefit users
  • Let us know if you've seen similar features in other apps

Stay Updated

  • Enable push notifications in our apps
