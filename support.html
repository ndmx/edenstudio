Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Open the ParkMemoryHub app, go to Settings > Account > Reset Password. Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings or by contacting us directly. All your data will be permanently removed.

Does the app work offline?

Basic photo capture and editing work offline. Location features and sharing require internet connectivity.

How do I export my photos?

In the app, select photos and choose "Export" from the share menu. Photos can be saved to your device or shared via email.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your photos are stored securely and only accessible by you and your authorized family members.
