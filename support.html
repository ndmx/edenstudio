Common Issues

  • Login Problems: Clear cache or reset password via email.
  • Sharing Fails: Check family code and internet; ensure permissions granted.
  • Location Errors: Enable in device settings (Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Park Memories).
  • Photo Uploads: Supported formats: JPEG, PNG; max 10MB.
