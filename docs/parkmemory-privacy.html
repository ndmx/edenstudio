1. Overview

ParkMemoryHub ("the App") is designed to help families capture, organize, and share precious memories in a secure, private environment. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and handle your personal information when you use our application.

Key Privacy Principles

  • Family-First Design: Built specifically for family sharing with privacy controls
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for app functionality
  • User Control: You maintain full control over your memories and sharing preferences
  • Transparency: Clear, understandable privacy practices

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile photo
  • Family Connections: Family member invitations and relationships
  • Memory Content: Photos, captions, location tags, timestamps
  • App Settings: Privacy preferences, notification settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: iOS version, device model, app version
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage, session duration, crash reports
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (only when explicitly enabled)
  • Camera Data: Photo metadata, filter preferences

2.3 Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for backend services, which may collect additional technical data as outlined in their privacy policy. All data is processed in accordance with our data processing agreements.

3. How We Use Your Data

Core Functionality

  • Enable photo capture and editing
  • Facilitate family sharing
  • Provide location-based features
  • Sync data across devices

App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Develop new features
  • Optimize performance

Communication

  • Send important app updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Notify about family activity
  • Share security alerts

Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws
  • Respond to legal requests
  • Protect user safety
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

Limited Sharing Scenarios:

  • Family Members: Content you choose to share within your family circle
  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google for backend infrastructure (under strict data processing agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect user safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (with user notification)

8. Children's Privacy

ParkMemoryHub is designed for family use and may be used by children under parental supervision. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • Parental Consent: Children under 13 require verifiable parental consent
  • Limited Data Collection: We collect minimal data from children necessary for app functionality
  • No Behavioral Advertising: We do not use children's data for advertising purposes
  • Parental Controls: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information

COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States where Firebase servers are located. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with service providers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Additional safeguards as required by local law

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify users of material changes through the app
  • Post the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Provide additional notice for significant changes
  • Maintain previous versions for reference
