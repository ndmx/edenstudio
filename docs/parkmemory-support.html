Download & Install

Download ParkMemoryHub from the App Store. Requires iOS 18.0 or later.

Set up your personal account to start capturing memories.

Set Up Permissions

Grant necessary permissions for full app functionality.

3. Common Issues & Solutions

üîê Login Problems

Symptoms: Cannot sign in, forgot password, account locked

Solutions:

  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset your password
  • Check your email for verification messages
  • Ensure you're using the correct email address
  • Clear app cache: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > ParkMemoryHub > Offload App

üì§ Photo Upload Failures

Symptoms: Photos won't upload, sharing fails, stuck on "uploading"

Solutions:

  • Check internet connection (WiFi or cellular)
  • Ensure photo is under 10MB file size limit
  • Supported formats: JPEG, PNG
  • Try uploading from a different location with better signal
  • Force close and restart the app

üìç Location Issues

Symptoms: Location not detected, incorrect location, radar not working

Solutions:

  • Enable location services: Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • Set ParkMemoryHub to "While Using App" or "Always"
  • Enable "Precise Location" for better accuracy
  • Check if location services are enabled for the device

Symptoms: Can't invite family, invitation not received, can't see family memories

Solutions:

  • Verify family member's email address is correct
  • Check spam/junk folders for invitation emails
  • Ensure family member has the app installed
  • Check family group privacy settings
  • Try regenerating the family invitation code

üîÑ Sync Issues

Symptoms: Memories not syncing, outdated content, missing photos

Solutions:

  • Pull down on memory timeline to refresh
  • Check internet connection stability
  • Sign out and sign back in to refresh sync

4. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile > Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days, except for backups which are retained for 6 months for recovery purposes.

Is my family's data private and secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely via Firebase. Only your designated family members can see shared memories. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Can I use ParkMemoryHub without location services?

Yes, location services are optional. You can still capture, edit, and share photos without enabling location. However, you won't be able to use location-based features like the Memory Radar or automatic location tagging.

How many family members can I add?

You can add up to 20 family members to your family group. This includes parents, children, grandparents, and extended family members.

What happens if I lose my phone?

Your memories are safely stored in the cloud. Simply install ParkMemoryHub on your new device and sign in with your account to access all your family memories.

Can children under 13 use the app?

Yes, with parental consent. We comply with COPPA regulations and require verifiable parental consent for children under 13. Parents maintain control over their child's account and data.

How do I export my memories?

Go to Profile > Settings > Data > Export Memories. You can download all your photos and captions in standard formats (JPEG for photos, JSON for metadata).

Why do I need to grant camera and photo permissions?

Camera permission is required to capture new photos within the app. Photo library permission allows you to save edited photos and access existing photos for sharing. These permissions are essential for the app's core functionality.

6. Family Group Management

Creating and Managing Family Groups

Creating a Family Group

  1. Go to Profile > Family > Create New Family
  2. Choose a family name and description
  3. Set privacy and sharing preferences
  4. Generate invitation codes for family members

Inviting Family Members

  1. Tap "Invite Family Member" in family settings
  2. Enter their email address or share invitation code
  3. Set their role (Admin, Member, or Child)
  4. They'll receive an email invitation to join

Managing Family Roles

  • Admin: Can invite/remove members, manage settings
  • Member: Can share memories and view family content
  • Child: Limited permissions, parental oversight

8. Technical Requirements

System Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 18.0 or later
  • Storage: Minimum 100MB free space
  • Internet: WiFi or cellular data connection
  • Camera: Built-in camera required for photo capture

Supported Devices

  • iPhone 12 and later (recommended)
  • iPhone 11 series (supported)
  • iPhone XS/XR series (supported)
  • iPad Pro, iPad Air, iPad (9th generation and later)

Network Requirements

  • Upload: Minimum 1 Mbps for photo sharing
  • Download: Minimum 2 Mbps for viewing family memories
  • Latency: Under 100ms for optimal real-time features
