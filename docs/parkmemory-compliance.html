2. Safety Guidelines (Section 1)

1.1 Objectionable Content

Our Implementation:

  • Family-focused content sharing with built-in moderation
  • No user-generated content visible to strangers
  • Private family circles prevent inappropriate exposure
  • Reporting mechanisms for inappropriate content

1.2 User Generated Content

Our Implementation:

  • Content limited to family photo sharing
  • No public forums or open content sharing
  • Family administrators can moderate content
  • Clear community guidelines for family sharing

1.3 Kids Category

Our Implementation:

  • COPPA-compliant data handling for children under 13
  • Parental consent mechanisms
  • No behavioral advertising to children
  • Age-appropriate interface design
